Premium Materials

Our semi-custom guitars are built using high-quality wood that we source and supply. Since each instrument is built by a team of three trained luthiers, we take advantage of traditional techniques by hand but also use CNC machines to make accurate and consistent cuts.

Shipping

We ship your instrument fully insured for the amount you paid, and only use the best packing materials and custom boxes we can find. You will receive automatic tracking updates via the email you provide during the order process

Quality Assurance

Each instrument must pass through a quality control checklist here in the USA to ensure it meets our high standards. We will also set up your guitar in your desired tuning/gauge of strings.
